Our Curriculum

A structured journey from digital native to technology professional.

Stage 1: The Foundation

Core programming logic using Python. Understanding variables, data structures, and algorithmic thinking.

Stage 2: Choice of Path

Learners evaluate their interests and select between the AI Engineering track or the Cybersecurity track.

Stage 3: Advanced Training

Intensive specialized modules focusing on neural networks (AI) or offensive/defensive operations (Security).

Stage 4: Project & Portfolio

Build real-world applications or security audits to demonstrate mastery and prepare for the industry.
